Purely Christmas – Website Design and Development

Purely Christmas love the festive season! The business was founded by Judy Sellwood, who has spent 15 years in retail. Judy wanted to offer Christmas decorations that capture the magic and joy of the season. Purely Christmas engaged Confetti Design to build an e-commerce website that distinguished their business. Judy wanted her customers to browse individual Christmas decorations or to purchase one of her specially curated Christmas decorating sets.

VISIT WEBSITE

The Strategy

Confetti Design was engaged to design and build the website including all product information pages. We designed and built individual shop pages that included variations, given the large number of products on offer. We worked dynamically with Judy, who was active in populating the product with content as the pages were built. The website was developed to enable the upload of one product per category that included product descriptions and product variations, with the shop sorting functions available for colour and material. This enabled Confetti Design to launch the website within four weeks. Confetti Design developed a mega menu dropdown for better product navigation for customers. This e-commerce website was integrated with a shopping cart and payment gateway.
The team at Confetti Design has built a large number of e-commerce websites and we know the benefit keyword analysis and research. The engagement with Confetti Design include keyword research for the Christmas products, including a list of recommended keywords for both the website content and for on-going blogging to achieve ranking goals. The site map that was recommended included recommendations for ranking including page URLs. Meta data was created for the core information pages and descriptions for Yoast included.

Schepisi Communications – Website Development

Schepisi Communications is an accredited Telstra Platinum partner that provides brand and whole of business telecommunications solutions to many of Telstra’s major and SME customers. Schepisi Communications partners with its clients to deliver telephony solutions across the range of Telstra products and services. These solutions include mobile management, data network solutions, collaboration products and services and cloud transition services.

VISIT WEBSITE

The Strategy

Nearly three years ago Schepisi Communications engaged its SEO company to re-design its large corporate website. Although the site had been designed, it was never built. As time passed it was evident a smaller more compact website that would act as a better communication tool to succinctly detail its product offering.

Confetti Design was engaged to work with the original design and layout that was detailed for the large corporate website and present this digital offering in a more compact fashion. The complexity of this assignment was to ensure that all of the original, large corporate website pages were still developed and included ‘behind the scene’ of the newly created micro-site to ensure the ongoing SEO integrity.

The Result

Both sites look seemless and if an indexed URL for the original site is clicked it takes the visitor to the new look brand but once they click the navigation they see only the micro site. This was a complex project as there were over 140 inner ‘hidden’ pages and yet the whole project was completed within 8 weeks.

My Lollies – Photography & Art Direction, Customised Website Design and Development

My Lollies is an innovative business that supplies Australia families and businesses with lollies. Offering more than 300 confectionary products that are consumed for kid’s birthday parties, fundraising and corporate giveaways, My Lollies has been continually growing due to the customer focused nature of the business.

The original website for My Lollies was developed using X-Cart, which is an ecommerce website software that facilitates the shopping process. As X-Cart was no longer going to be supported by its developers, the team at My Lollies needed to move to a new platform to facilitate the shopping experience for its customers.

VISIT WEBSITE

The Strategy

The team at My Lollies had originally tried to develop a new website utilising an overseas developer and using the Magento ecommerce platform. The process to deliver the new website had stalled with the new site not presenting as well as hoped.

Confetti Design was engaged with the goal to showcase the extensive suite of My Lollies products, together with the business owners. The focus of the new website was to celebrate its core product of customised lolly bags and to showcase the business owners of My Lollies.

The engagement with Confetti Design included new branding photography and art direction. The complexity of this custom build was the export of the 9000 customers, 20000 order history and 400 products from X-Cart. As an online site it had to be fast, easy to order and checkout, accurate shipping costs and also multiple discount or free offers.

The Result

The site is super fast, easy to navigation and showcases the brand and products. The images were taken by Fi Mims Photography and reinforce the brand’s fun, vibrant values.

Dora – Website Design and Development

Since 1994 Dora International have been combining the finest gold, platinum, palladium, titanium and diamonds to create men’s wedding bands and rings that unite modern design with timeless style. Their rings marry the latest technology with true hand craftsmanship. With a commitment to crafting only the finest quality rings, Dora provides quality men’s wedding rings to over 450 stockists around Australia and New Zealand. The parent company, based in New York also supplies stockist all around the world.

VISIT WEBSITE

The Strategy

Dora is a wholesaler to stockists, but was wanting to create brand loyalty where couples only ask for a “Dora ring” when in store. Dora was looking to support its stockists, and to build the Dora brand in mind of the end user – it did not want to build an online store.
The existing website was hindered by a large range of rings and overwhelming to navigate. The new website for Dora needed to present a more streamlined way of viewing its extensive collections and showcasing the quality of the rings as well as the lifetime of customer service it offers the purchasers.
The Dora website we designed and built included “real” wedding photography images sourced were used as part of the site. The tactic to achieve the enhanced brand awareness goal was to build out the positioning statement of “Dora means more”, showcasing the lifetime warranty and ring replacement policy offered with every ring sold. The engagement with Confetti Design extended to redesigning the warranty card, printing and linking the warranty registration to the Dora website and the internal CRM. This strategy had the benefit to enable Dora to further market to an audience they had not previously had contact with.
