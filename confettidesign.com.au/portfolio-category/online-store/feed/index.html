My Lollies – Photography & Art Direction, Customised Website Design and Development

My Lollies is an innovative business that supplies Australia families and businesses with lollies. Offering more than 300 confectionary products that are consumed for kid’s birthday parties, fundraising and corporate giveaways, My Lollies has been continually growing due to the customer focused nature of the business.

The original website for My Lollies was developed using X-Cart, which is an ecommerce website software that facilitates the shopping process. As X-Cart was no longer going to be supported by its developers, the team at My Lollies needed to move to a new platform to facilitate the shopping experience for its customers.

VISIT WEBSITE

The Strategy

The team at My Lollies had originally tried to develop a new website utilising an overseas developer and using the Magento ecommerce platform. The process to deliver the new website had stalled with the new site not presenting as well as hoped.

Confetti Design was engaged with the goal to showcase the extensive suite of My Lollies products, together with the business owners. The focus of the new website was to celebrate its core product of customised lolly bags and to showcase the business owners of My Lollies.

The engagement with Confetti Design included new branding photography and art direction. The complexity of this custom build was the export of the 9000 customers, 20000 order history and 400 products from X-Cart. As an online site it had to be fast, easy to order and checkout, accurate shipping costs and also multiple discount or free offers.

The Result

The site is super fast, easy to navigation and showcases the brand and products. The images were taken by Fi Mims Photography and reinforce the brand’s fun, vibrant values.

Fizz Kidz – Website Design & Development

Fizz Kidz are focused on empowering children to be their own ‘champions of choice’, providing programs that focused on hands-on fun that enable kids to make their own creations from scratch. Kizz Kidz offers in-store and mobile parties, holiday and after school programs.

VISIT WEBSITE

The Strategy

The team at Fizz Kidz were happy with their existing branding and needed the website to remain consistent with that branding. The design brief for the website was to present a site that was both playful and colourful, given the nature of their service offering. There was a complexity to the website as an online booking form was required as part of the development. In many instances, one parent might be booking a party for a number of kids with an understanding of the child’s specific needs, such as allergies.

Miei Fiori – Melbourne Florist Delivery | Melbourne Corporate Florist

Lyly and Rob approached me with a plan of where they are taking their social enterprise. business. They shared their purpose and why. We worked together and pulled in other experts Lin Fong for copywriting and Fi Mims Photography for images to create a beautiful website that will take them forward in achieving incredible goals for reducing poverty in Australia using flowers as the vehicle.

VISIT WEBSITE

The Strategy

Miei Fiori is a social enterprise so it was vital the website update explained this value proposition. Lyly and Rob are not simply selling bunches of flowers. They are providing a percentage of profits to their charity of choice, The Smith Family. We needed to communicate the transparency around the costing structure and also how it worked so individuals and businesses will buy into the social enterprise. To achieve this we created graphics to match their logo. We assisted in also designing the skin for the delivery van.
