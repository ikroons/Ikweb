Scope of Works

During the project briefing, it is client’s responsibility to provide us with clear guidelines along with the flow or specific details you may require. When such details are not provided, we will proceed with our understanding of your requirements and quote accordingly. At a later stage, if a discrepancy arises, it may lead to additional costs to accommodate the changes. This includes additional pages to the base cost quoted. Additional page costs may range from $400 ex GST to $75 ex GST subject to the content and amount of design or development required.
Thus, it is essential that you clarify every aspect of your website development and ensure that you have been quoted on the right requirements.

Once the quote has been finalised, any additions, changes or enhancements in the functionality or design of the project will affect the proposal and may incur additional cost and a revised delivery date. All additional work, over and above the estimates is charged separately. Under no circumstances will Confetti Design be liable for any delays caused by change in the project brief.

Any complexity related to specific deliverable, must be advised in advance and included in the proposal for costing purposes. We operate in good faith and rely on our clients to disclose the full picture of their expectations. Any discrepancy arising due to unclear requirements or expectations will not be borne by Confetti Design.

Website content and all related materials need to be provided to us within the first four weeks of starting the project, unless otherwise agreed. Any delays thereafter may delay the project and may incur additional charges if it goes beyond reasonable timeframe, however we make every effort to keep projects on time even in event of client’s content delivery delay if we have the capacity to do so. Confetti Design will inform if a delay will impact delivery schedules.

The client must recognize that at times there may be unforeseen circumstances that will delay the development process, particularly with reference to the integration of third party software or unforeseen schedule interruptions. We will try our best to complete the project as agreed in the proposal. As long as it is within a reasonable period, the client agrees not to penalize us for any genuine delay, when every effort to keep the project on the proposed schedule is taken.

Upon approval of project designs and payment for building stage Confetti Design allows for 2 rounds of changes to the development site. Any changes outside of background colour, copy or image will incur extra costs. Any deviations from the approved designs will incur extra costs.

Domain registration charges are not included as a part of the proposal unless mentioned otherwise. If domain registration is part of the project, the renewal of that domain will be charged in time to keep the domain and then invoiced to client.

Website Hosting

Hosting charges are not included in the quotations unless mentioned otherwise.
Confetti Design offers managed WordPress hosting that includes an automatic SSL certificate. Hosting is paid for by subscription. It does not include website security.

The client may wish to organise their own hosting, and it is their responsibility to keep this hosting account active, not that of Confetti Design. Where clients decide to organise their own hosting, we should be consulted before finalising the type of hosting and database, as it should meet the requirements of the technology used for the development.
Please note that we’ll require full access with hosting support for testing and deploying the application.

Confetti Design will not be liable for any delays or errors caused by direct or indirect actions of the hosting company.
Confetti Design cannot give access to their test servers and test websites to the clients or any third party. Please note that these support hours are not applicable if the design or coding or any other aspect of the website has been altered by anyone other than the Confetti Design team.

All prices are quoted in Australian dollars are exclusive of GST unless specified.
All invoices are to be processed as per the schedule via your preferred method of payment. Milestone payments must be made within 5 working days of each milestone/schedule, unless mutually agreed otherwise.
Final payment must be made prior to website going live or being moved to client allocated hosting.

  • Receipt of payment is deemed as acceptance of the quote, milestone, delivery or project.
  • Confetti Design reserves the right to negotiate and refund appropriate portion of the amount paid by customer towards the requested service.
  • If you cancel your order after making the payment, but the work has not commenced, we will refund 75% of the payment that you have already made.
  • If the order is cancelled after the commencement of work, the maximum refund or charge will be 50% of the invoice.
  • There will be no refund or adjustments if cancellation is communicated when more than 50% of the work has been completed.
  • All cancellations must be received in writing and can be sent via email.
  • Telephone requests on cancellations will not be accepted. For minor or once-off works, payments need to be made either in advance or as per the terms mentioned in the invoice/proposal.
  • All works completed on an existing site are to paid for up front with 50% and then 50% upon completion, unless otherwise agreed.
  • If invoices are not attended to within 10 working days a Maintenance page will be put up until payment is received.
  • If you require any changes to the terms, you must inform us in writing (emails accepted) within two working days of the receipt of the same.

Confetti Design will not be liable for any costs or charges associated with the recovery of the outstanding amount. Any charges associated with the recovery of the outstanding amount (debt collectors, administration charges, legal fees) will be referred to the client for processing. If you are late with a payment or have any queries regarding the Invoice, please contact us immediately on receiving the invoice or reminder emails.
All communications/correspondences is generally done via emails. It is client’s responsibility to keep us updated with their relevant email addresses.
Any payment relating to the domain name registration renewal or hosting or any 3rd party products or service purchased on behalf of the client will have to be in full and is non-refundable.
If client chooses a 3rd party hosting provider prior to starting a project, Confetti Design will build and test the website on one of our own servers or hosted domains. The website may be transferred-off to the nominated 3rd party server upon full payment of all invoices and dues.
